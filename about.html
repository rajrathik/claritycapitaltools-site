What This Is

Clarity Capital Tools is a platform of free, data-driven financial analysis tools for everyday investors. Every tool runs on real data — no synthetic returns, no back-fitted curves, no marketing gloss.

The flagship suite lives at investsim.claritycapitaltools.com and includes 18 tools covering portfolio backtesting, Monte Carlo simulation, S&P 500 historical analysis, sector analytics, crash recovery analysis, and more.

The Data

Historical S&P 500 tools are powered by the Robert Shiller dataset from Yale Economics — 1,863 rows of monthly data from January 1871 to today. This is the same dataset used in academic research on long-term market returns.

Sector ETF tools use Yahoo Finance price and dividend data. Money market benchmarks use the FRED federal funds rate — the actual risk-free rate, month by month.

Simulation rule that matters: Every portfolio simulation buys at the monthly high price — the worst-case entry point. No cherry-picking. If a strategy looks good here, it's because it's genuinely good.

Built Entirely With AI

Here's the part that surprises most people: not a single line of code in this platform was written by hand.

Every file — the database schema, the FastAPI backend, the vanilla JavaScript frontend, the Canvas charts, the Auth0 authentication, the PostgreSQL deployment, the data pipeline from Yahoo Finance and FRED — was written entirely by AI through conversation.

The work was directing, reviewing, and making every product decision. Knowing what to build and why it should work a certain way. Knowing when to push back on what the AI suggested. That turned out to be the real skill.

It's an honest proof of what's possible when you apply AI as a force multiplier rather than a shortcut.

What We're Building Toward

Clarity Capital Tools is growing. The goal is a comprehensive financial toolkit covering:

  • Portfolio analysis & backtesting (live now)
  • Retirement planning & Monte Carlo simulation (live now)
  • Tax-aware investment analysis

  • AI-powered financial Q&A grounded in real data

Every tool will follow the same principles: real data, honest methodology, free to use, no noise.

The Mission

The financial services industry makes money by making investing feel complicated. The data tells a simpler story — if you know where to look.

This platform exists to put that data in the hands of people who want to think for themselves.
