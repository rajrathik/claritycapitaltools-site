Built to Answer the Questions
Financial Tools Usually Dodge

Most financial tools rely on polished projections and tidy assumptions. Clarity Capital Tools takes a different approach — using real historical data to help investors see how markets and portfolios have actually behaved, including the difficult periods many tools smooth over.

What This Is

Clarity Capital Tools is a platform of data-driven financial analysis tools designed for everyday investors. The focus is practical: helping people explore risk, returns, portfolio behavior, and long-term outcomes with greater clarity.

Built with AI, this platform was shaped by a view of markets that extends beyond clean models and tidy assumptions — closer to the questions investors actually ask and the outcomes they actually live with. It brings together a growing suite of tools designed to turn more than 150 years of market history into clearer insight for real-world investing.

The flagship suite at investsim.claritycapitaltools.com includes tools for portfolio backtesting, Monte Carlo simulation, S&P 500 historical analysis, sector analytics, crash recovery analysis, and more.

Real Data. Honest Methodology.

Historical market tools draw on the Robert Shiller dataset from Yale Economics, with monthly data stretching from January 1871 to today. Sector analysis uses Yahoo Finance price and dividend data, while cash benchmarks use the FRED federal funds rate.

The methodology is intentionally conservative. Portfolio simulations assume purchases at the monthly high price — a worst-case entry point rather than an idealized one. No curve fitting. No cherry-picking. No marketing gloss.

Simulation rule that matters: Every portfolio simulation buys at the monthly high price — the worst-case entry point. If a strategy looks good here, it's because it's genuinely good.

What It's For

Clarity Capital Tools is being built as a broader financial toolkit for investors who want something more grounded than standard calculators and sales-driven projections. The goal is to make market history more useful, portfolio analysis more honest, and financial decision-making more informed.

The Mission

Investing is often made to look more complicated than it needs to be. The data tells a clearer story — when it is presented honestly.

Clarity Capital Tools exists to put that story in the hands of people who want to think for themselves.
